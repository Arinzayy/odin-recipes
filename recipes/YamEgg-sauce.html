Yam-Egg Sauce

Description

Yam-egg sauce is a Nigerian food, it can be eaten at any time of the day. it's easy to make and taste yummy when prepared well.

Ingredients

  • Half tuber of yam
  • 4 eggs
  • 200ml vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup of sliced onions
  • 2 tsp of crayfish
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • Chopped pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Slice yam,Put yam in a pan, add enough water to cover it, add salt and place on medium heat and cook till soft.
  2. Slice your tomatoes, onions, and peppers using a kitchen knife.
  3. Set frying pan on the heat and add just 200ml of oil (vegetable or olive oil), allow the oil to heat up for few minutes.
  4. Add the sliced tomatoes, peppers, onions and fry.
  5. Add salt to taste,a seasoning cube. Stir to avoid burning.
  6. Beat eggs in a separate bowl, add a pinch of salt and pour it into the frying tomatoes.
  7. Allow the egg to fry.
  8. Don't let it get burned. Try breaking into tiny crumbs.
  9. Serve egg sauce with boiled white yams.
